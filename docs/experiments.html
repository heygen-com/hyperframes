The experiments

Ten more candidates: the texture registers from Codrops, gmunk's machine vision, the type crafts, and the time laws. Watch, then choose.

ascii-render-pass

the texture king

A scene renders as living ASCII, resolving from coarse glyph noise to a legible fine grid.

ordered-dither-pass

premium lofi

Bayer dithering carries the scene from pure 2-tone noise to clean, or dissolves it the other way.

segmentation-flood

after gmunk

Machine-vision masks flood the subject in scanline steps, brackets and labels landing per region.

slit-scan-reveal

time as material

Every row of the frame lives at a different moment; the subject smears through time until now catches up.

variable-font-flex

breathing type

A word lands by inflating through real weight and width axes while its box never moves.

stitched-text-draw

embroidered

Letters sew themselves on: stitch dashes, a leading needle, a thread tail that settles.

ink-bleed-reveal

liquid

Ink blobs bloom through paper, merge like liquid, and resolve into the mark.

particle-text-dissolve

both directions

Text condenses from a particle cloud behind a wipe front, or scatters back into one.

modal-morph

the open-it-up beat

A small card grows into a full panel, its contents re-flowing continuously the whole way.

stop-motion-cadence

hand-made time

A collage throw at 8-12 quantized frames per second: squash on hits, boil on edges, sparkles that live three frames.
